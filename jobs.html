Current Opportunities

At ShopSphere, we build customer-focused online retail experiences that make shopping simpler, faster, and more accessible. As our platform continues to grow, we are looking for talented people who can support website development, product presentation, and user application workflows.

Digital Development

Front-End Web Developer

We are seeking a Front-End Web Developer to support the improvement of our customer-facing retail website. This role focuses on building accessible, responsive, and visually consistent web pages for product browsing and online application workflows at ShopSphere.

Ref: FWD25

Salary: AUD 78,000 - AUD 92,000 per year

Reports to: Digital Development Manager

Key Responsibilities

  1. Develop and maintain HTML and CSS for website pages.
  2. Improve page layout, navigation, and responsive behaviour.
  3. Support product listing templates and promotional landing pages.
  4. Work with designers to implement consistent brand styling.
  5. Check pages for accessibility, usability, and cross-browser compatibility.

Essential Requirements

  • Strong knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Understanding of responsive web design principles.
  • Ability to create clear and semantic page structures.
  • Good attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience with e-commerce or retail websites.
  • Familiarity with accessibility standards and inclusive design.
  • Basic understanding of GitHub or version control workflows.
  • Experience working with designers or content teams.

User Experience & Content

UX Content & Product Listing Coordinator

We are looking for a UX Content & Product Listing Coordinator to improve the clarity and usability of product information across our online retail platform. This role supports product content quality, customer journey consistency, and application form usability at ShopSphere.

Ref: UXC25

Salary: AUD 65,000 - AUD 76,000 per year

Reports to: User Experience Lead

Key Responsibilities

  1. Prepare and update product descriptions and listing content.
  2. Review website text for clarity, consistency, and tone.
  3. Support improvements to application and enquiry forms.
  4. Work with the UX team to make content easy to scan and understand.
  5. Help ensure online information is accurate and customer-friendly.

Essential Requirements

  • Strong written communication skills.
  • Ability to organise information clearly and accurately.
  • Understanding of user-focused digital content.
  • Good teamwork and time management skills.
  • Confidence using web-based systems and online platforms.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience in e-commerce, retail, or digital marketing.
  • Awareness of accessibility and inclusive communication.
  • Experience editing website or catalogue content.
  • Interest in customer experience and online shopping behaviour.
